How it works

rockholt.org provides email forwarding only. Messages sent to your @rockholt.org address are delivered to the destination email address you choose.

1) Choose your address

Pick something simple like firstname@rockholt.org or a shared address like family@rockholt.org.

2) Tell us where to forward

We point your forwarding address to the inbox you already use (Gmail, iCloud, work email, etc.).

3) Use it anywhere

Give out your @rockholt.org address. Mail will arrive in your existing inbox automatically.

Important notes

  • Forwarding is delivery only—there is no mailbox, login, or storage on rockholt.org.
  • Your destination inbox provider stores messages and handles most spam filtering.
  • If you need a full mailbox later, visit rockholt.com.
Forwarding is provided via Cloudflare Email Routing.
