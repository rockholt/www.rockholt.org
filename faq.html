FAQ

Answers to common questions about forwarding-only addresses.

Is this a real mailbox?

No. rockholt.org is forwarding-only. There is no inbox, login, or storage on rockholt.org.

Where does the mail go?

Mail is forwarded to the destination email address you provide (Gmail, iCloud, work email, etc.).

Can I change where it forwards?

Yes. Email admin@rockholt.com and we can update the destination address.

Can I send mail from my @rockholt.org address?

Forwarding addresses are designed for receiving mail. Sending from an @rockholt.org address depends on your mailbox provider and configuration. If you need a full mailbox with sending built-in, use rockholt.com.

What if I want a full inbox later?

Upgrade anytime at rockholt.com for a managed mailbox solution.
