Privacy

Plain-language privacy commitments for rockholt.org forwarding.

We collect the minimum information needed to provide forwarding: the forwarding address and the destination email address you choose.

Forwarding-only: rockholt.org does not provide a mailbox or storage. Messages are delivered to your chosen destination inbox provider.

We do not sell personal information.
